Our History

Beistegui Hermanos S.A. company, more commonly known as BH, is not only one of the storied bike makers, but also the largest Bike Trainer supplier in Europe.With nearly 100 years of experience building bikes, no matter indoor or outdoor, we always believe that providing the best riding experience for cyclists is a top priority.

Year 1909

Beistegui Hermanos S.A. company was founded, more commonly knows as BH, initially dedicated to the weapons industry with offering high-precision weapon.

Year 1919

With the World War I now over and having gained significant experience in steel treatments, BH started manufactures completed bicycle and currently became the largest bicycle brand in Spain.

Year 1969

BH Group started BH fitness business with extending the technology of precise steel treatment and combining electronics and machinery technology.

Year 1975

BH officially launched the first exercise machine with BH brand name, specifically static exercise bicycles.

Year 1985

The company Exercycle S.A. was formed with the aim of centering production on exercise appliances and responding to the growing demand for this type of equipment.

Year 1987

BH Fitness was the first company to introduce the electronic monitor on its product for Europe.

Year 1988

BH Fitness introduced a new adjustable hydraulic system for rowing machine.

Year 1990

BH Fitness was the first company with monitors to provide simultaneous readout of basic functions (distance, calories burned, time and speed).

Year 1992

BH Fitness launched the first home exercise bicycle with a magnetic braking system.

Year 1995

BH applied aluminum alloy for home products for better reliability and less weight.

Year 1996

BH launched the first European manufactured spin bike. Nowadays, BH is the largest spinning bike suppliers in Europe.

Exercycle S.A. began manufacturing the Hi-Powe brand of fitness equipment for the professional sector. Exercycle S.A. launched an AC motor with variable speed control for professional treadmills, providing greater reliability and redefined the difference between home use standard and commercial standard.

Year 1997

A third exercise equipment factory was established in Portugal: Maquinasport Aparelhos de Desporto, S.A. In the same year, the forth exercise equipment factory was established in Portugal: Exer Fitness, S.A. Furthermore, BH brand also covered the whole Europe and South America and plan to promote the brand step by step to Asia and North America.

Year 1998

BH officially established the health and massage brand, BH SHIATSU. Based on the foundation of innovation for health, BH SHIATSU expected to bring healthy life to our customers around the world.

Year 2006

BH Asia Ltd. was formed in Taiwan to serve the customers in East, Southeast, South Asian and Pan-Pacific countries.

Year 2007

BH Fitness officially acquired a California based fitness company, Bladez Fitness to set up BH North America Corporation, covering the whole North American market.

BH China was officially formed in Shanghai City, China, establishing the business network in Asia and offer high quality products and service to whole Asia.

Year 2009

BH celebrated 100 years old birthday and wishes to devote more for people's health in the following 100 years.

Year 2010

Green Company BH group was the first one to be certified in accordance with the ISO 14001 standards.

Year 2011

As a green company, we apply ECO mode technology which reduces an average of 25% power consumption in the control software.

Year 2012

2012 London, Olympic champion, Nicola Spirig, sponsored by BH

Year 2014

BH is the first fitness brand officially licensed by NBA
